About Us

Mwanzo Bora (“Good Beginning”) is a community-based organization in Kibera, Nairobi, dedicated to driving positive change by addressing critical social challenges. Our work focuses on sexual and reproductive health and rights. Through community dialogues, practical interventions, and strong partnerships, we strive to build a future where everyone has equal opportunities, secure rights, and the support they need to thrive—ensuring no one is left behind.

What We Do

We Are in A Mission To Bring
Change To The Community

Our Objectives

  • - Improve access to sexual and reproductive health information and services for youth and communities
  • - Prevent and respond to sexual and gender-based violence through awareness, support, and advocacy
  • - Strengthen child protection systems to prevent and address exploitation and abuse
  • - Promote climate action and environmental sustainability through community engagement and youth empowerment
  • - Empower communities through education, dialogue, and inclusive, community-led initiatives
