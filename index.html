About Us

We're worldwide non-profit charity ogranization.

Mwanzo Bora (“Good Beginning”) is a community-based organization in Kibera, Nairobi, dedicated to driving positive change by addressing critical social challenges. Our work focuses on sexual and reproductive health and rights. Through community dialogues, practical interventions, and strong partnerships, we strive to build a future where everyone has equal opportunities, secure rights, and the support they need to thrive—ensuring no one is left behind.

What We Are Doing

We Are in A Mission To Bring
Change To The Community

Sexual Reproductive Health Rights(SRHR)

We provide youth-friendly information and services to empower individuals to make informed decisions about their bodies and health

Sexual Gender Based Violence (SGBV)

We work to prevent and respond to all forms of gender-based violence through community awareness, survivor support, and advocacy

Child Sexual Exploitation and Abuse

We are committed to safeguarding children and creating environments where they are protected and heard.

Climate Action

We empower communities to link environmental health with human wellbeing through sustainable practices.
